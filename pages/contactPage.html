Frequently Asked Questions

Find quick answers to common questions about our school.

What are the school hours?

Our regular school hours are from 7:45 AM to 2:30 PM, Monday through Friday. After-school programs and tutoring sessions run until 5:00 PM on designated days.

How can I schedule a campus tour?

To schedule a campus tour, please contact our Admissions Office at admissions@stteresa.edu.za or call +27 43 555 0124. Tours are available Monday through Friday between 9:00 AM and 2:00 PM.

What documents are required for admission?

Required documents include a completed application form, certified copy of birth certificate, previous school reports, transfer card (if applicable), immunization records, and two recent passport-sized photographs.

Does the school offer financial assistance?

Yes, we offer need-based financial assistance to qualifying families. Applications for financial aid are considered annually and require submission of supporting financial documentation. Contact our Finance Office for more details.

How do I report my child's absence?

Please notify the school office by phone (+27 43 555 0123) or email (attendance@stteresa.edu.za) before 8:30 AM on the day of absence. For extended absences, a written note is required upon return to school.
