Admissions Process

Begin your journey with St. Teresa Senior Secondary School. We're dedicated to providing quality education rooted in Catholic values to students from all backgrounds.

Join Our Academic Community

As a Quintile 1 institution, St. Teresa offers fee-free education to all qualifying students. Our admissions process is designed to be transparent, fair, and accessible to families throughout the Eastern Cape region.

January - March

Application Period

Submit your application forms along with required documents during our open application period. Late applications may be considered based on availability.

April

Assessment & Review

Our admissions committee reviews all applications. Selected candidates may be invited for placement assessments or interviews.

May

Admission Offers

Successful applicants receive admission offers via email and post. Parents must confirm acceptance within two weeks.

June - July

Orientation & Registration

New student orientation sessions and final registration processes take place. Uniform fittings and textbook distribution are completed.

Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our admissions process

What are the school fees for St. Teresa?

As a Quintile 1 school, St. Teresa provides fee-free education to all students. There are no tuition fees. Parents are only responsible for purchasing school uniforms and stationery supplies.

What is the application deadline?

The application period for the 2025 academic year opens on January 15, 2025 and closes on March 31, 2025. Late applications may be considered based on availability of spaces.

Are there any entrance exams?

We do not require entrance exams for Grade 8 admissions. For students applying to Grades 9-12, we review previous academic records and may request placement assessments in core subjects to determine appropriate class placement.

What support is available for students from disadvantaged backgrounds?

We offer several support programs including the National School Nutrition Program that provides daily meals, a textbook lending library, after-school tutoring, and counseling services. Limited financial assistance is available for uniforms through our community partnership program.

How will I know if my application has been successful?

All applicants will receive notification via email and SMS by May 15, 2025. Successful applicants will receive an acceptance letter with registration instructions. If you haven't received communication by May 20, please contact our admissions office.
